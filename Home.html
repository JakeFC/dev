About Me

Hi! I'm Jacob Chavera, a software engineer in the SF Bay Area about to complete my AR/VR specialization at Holberton School, Tulsa. Growing up, I was always interested in learning languages and math, as well as computer programs that served to feed my mind for strategy and theory-crafting. What started as the beginning of a coding career at Holberton has since become a passion that occasionally keeps me up at night.  From my very first javascript, I could tell creating interactive experiences was going to be the most rewarding thing I could do and my time with Unity has certainly confirmed that. Not only can you bring that experience directly into people's homes, but the creative freedom AR/VR technologies allow, as well as the constant learning that goes alongside emerging technologies like them are why I've decided to make a coding home in this virtual space.
